Cases of Ransomware and Holding onto files

Since 1990 when the term World WIde Web was coined by Tim Berners Lee there has been people and companies who have used the connected world to gain access to business/enterprise, government and personal computer systems often taking control of systems and not releasing until a fee is payed for.

Often when there is encryption in systems there are still other means in which attackers can get files, Commonly this is due to weak Passwords which may be used across many different websites and could be explited this way.
