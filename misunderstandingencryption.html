Common Misunderstandings

Despite Encryption being used in a variety of areas in IT, not everybody has a full understanding of what the advantages are and this can lead to consequences for businesses and ordinary people. Encryption by itself is powerful and can help safeguard systems, websites, servers and sensitive information.

Encryption is not foolproof and there are people who have the knowledge to be break through the systems and gain access to the information through a range of attacks, whether in person or by using a device capable of monitoring web traffic or gaining access to a personal computer with not strong enough protection applied.

Why Encrypt Files and Folders

Programming languages, browsers and remote desktop software allow People from anywhere with a internet connection to be able to access Information across different medium through accessing different parts of device activities. This makes information such as payslips, CV's and photo folders incredibly valuable for people with bad intentions.
